My Favorite features

Wave ramp

The wave ramp is the first thing that you see when you get to the skate park. It is a set of 3 smoothened bumps that allow you to build up speed and direct you back out through the park entrance. You get to it by going up on the second platform on the side opposite side of the park and then walking around the edge back towards the front. You can also just run up the top ramp. I use this ramp mostly for fun, but it is also great for learning how to distribute your body weight for increased speed.

Quarter pipe
